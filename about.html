The Story

I'm Kaan Guluzada, the founder of Guluzada Studio. I sit at the intersection of design and technology. With a background in both brand aesthetics and full-stack development, I bridge the gap that often exists between how a site looks and how it works.

I started this studio with a simple mission: to banish mediocre websites. Your website is often the first interaction a customer has with your brand. It should not only work; it should impress. Whether you need a simple landing page or a complex web application, I bring the same level of precision and passion to every pixel.
